Kikulacho was formed in 2020 and we have quickly become Nairobi’s premier destination for eco-friendly, farm-to-table dining. Our restaurant is more than just a place to eat; it’s a sanctuary that celebrates fresh farm produce, featuring an exquisite seasonal menu and sustainable gastronomy.

We take pride in offering organic heirloom crops, carefully cultivated to bring you the most exquisite flavours. Our commitment to sustainability extends to every aspect of our operation, from sourcing local, seasonal ingredients to supporting local entrepreneurs and nurturing young talents.

Step into our doors and embark on a culinary journey like no other. As you indulge in our seasonal menu, you become an integral part of our vibrant community, co-creating with local artisans and farmers who share our passion for flavourful, organic dining options.

Experience the joy of knowing that every bite you take supports ethical and eco-friendly practices.
